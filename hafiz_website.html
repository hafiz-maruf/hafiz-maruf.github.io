About

I am a Master in International Affairs candidate at the Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy, National University of Singapore — one of Asia's foremost institutions for governance and public policy education.

My work sits at the intersection of international relations, sustainable development, and geopolitical strategy, with a particular focus on ASEAN regional governance, disaster resilience, and the intersection of economic development and environmental sustainability.

I am passionate about translating policy ideas into real-world impact through rigorous research, stakeholder engagement, and international collaboration — connecting academic insight to the practitioners and institutions that shape our region.
