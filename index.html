Index Construction

Methodology

Fixed SADRI method

Five fixed pillars

SADRI uses five fixed pillars. Each pillar has equal weight: 20%. Pillar weights are not adjustable.

Official score

The official SADRI score is calculated as the average of the five pillar scores.

Score scale

All values are 0-100 index scores, not percentages. Higher scores always indicate stronger resilience.

Risk-based indicators

Indicators where higher raw values mean higher risk are reverse-coded before aggregation.

Sensitivity analysis

The sensitivity tool is exploratory and does not change the official fixed-weight SADRI score.
